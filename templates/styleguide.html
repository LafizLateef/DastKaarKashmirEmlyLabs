Motion & Interaction

  • Cards lift their shadow on hover (--shadow-hover); interactive cards (products, artisans, roles) also scale down slightly on press.
  • All buttons and clickable rows scale to .97 on :active for tactile feedback.
  • Page content fades/slides in on load; overview and grid cards stagger in sequentially.
  • A top progress bar animates on internal navigation clicks and cross-fades pages automatically in browsers that support the View Transitions API.
  • Everything above respects prefers-reduced-motion and collapses to instant, non-animated state.
